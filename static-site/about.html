Simplifying the search for senior living with comprehensive information, trusted insights, and personalized guidance for families across America.

Serving seniors since 2018

Our Story

Radiant Retirement was founded in 2018 by Sarah Johnson, a senior care professional with over 15 years of experience, who experienced firsthand the challenges of finding suitable housing for her aging parents. What began as a small directory of trusted facilities in Arizona has grown into a nationwide resource.

Our mission emerged from a simple observation: families needed a trusted, comprehensive source of information when making one of life's most important decisions. Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach our work with genuine empathy, understanding the emotional journey of finding senior care. Every recommendation is made with the care we would want for our own loved ones.

Integrity

We provide honest, unbiased information to help families make informed decisions. Our recommendations are never influenced by partnerships, and we maintain complete transparency in all operations.

Innovation

We continuously enhance our platforms and resources to provide the most user-friendly experience for seniors and their families. We embrace new technologies and methods to improve our service.

Community

We believe in the power of connection and building relationships with the families we serve. We're dedicated to creating a supportive network for seniors and their loved ones throughout their journey.
